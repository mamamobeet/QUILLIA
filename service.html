Let's Read

HOW TO BORROW BOOKS

  • Order anything from the café and keep your receipt.
  • Show the receipt to the Book Keeper at the book corner.
  • Choose a book and write your name in the logbook.
  • Receive a bookmark as proof of borrowing.
  • Books are for in-store reading only (1 book at a time).

HOW TO RATE BOOKS

  • Return the book to the Book Keeper.
  • Get a mini rating card (stars 1–5).
  • Rate the book; optional small review.
  • Staff encodes ratings to update the online catalog.
